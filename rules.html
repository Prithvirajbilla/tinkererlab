Rules

  1. Permission to use the lab will be granted only after a basic abstract of the work that the student wants to do in the lab is submitted. This abstract should include the following things:
    • A brief description of the planned work
    • The components required.
    • The tentative Deadlines for project completion.
    • Add technovation review team.
  2. Students will update their abstracts and fill in all the necessary details(mentioned in the first point) using their Tech-IDs.
  3. This Tech-ID will help us in keeping a constant track of the success of the projects done in the lab and will also encourage the healthy habit of maintaining a proper documentation of the work done.
  4. A database of the students who end up spoiling some components inside the lab would be maintained by the technician.
  5. At the end of every year interested students can file for the Tinkerers of the year award. The work done by these students can then be scrutinized by a committee (consisting of Alumni,Profs,Student In-charge et cetera). This can be easily done with the help of the documentation and the abstracts that the students would have submitted on their Tech-ID.
  6. Disciplinary action will be taken against the students if they are found violating any of the rules.
