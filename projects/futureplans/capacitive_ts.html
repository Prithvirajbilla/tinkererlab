Capacitive Touch Screen

Ever thought of making your own touch screen?

Here is a chance for you!

Components Required

  1. 555- timer
  2. Plethora of well calculated resistances
  3. Speakers
  4. Aluminium sheet
  5. Fabrication related stuff
  6. LEDs

Team Mentors

  1. Prajwal Raut

What will you learn?

Cool stuff can be created out of simple things, working of 555 timers, opamps, flipflops, the basic concepts behind a capacitive touch screen. If we take the project further then 7 segment displays and microcontrollers. You will get skills like soldering, circuit design, eagle,solidworks, product design. In addition to all the instruments you get to use in the process.

How will you achieve it?

We learned the astable mode of operation of 555 timer IC. In it we saw that the frequency of the output wave was given by that big scary formula which had a capacitance term in its denominator. Now, imagine a circuit where we use the high capacitance that our body provides to change the frequency of this timer. This circuit followed by a low pass filter can be used to understand the basic principles behind a capacitive touch screen, and that is exactly what this project is aiming for.

Further!!

Once we successfully finish this basic circuit, we will think of how to take this project further, one way would be in the direction of making a touch screen keypad of numbers, and we with the help of microcontrollers display it on an LCD display unit.This portion is open for discussion. :)
