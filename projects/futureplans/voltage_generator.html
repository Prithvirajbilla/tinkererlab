Voltage Generator

Curious to know how DC voltage supplies work?

Here is a chance for you to make one yourself!

Components Required

  1. Transformer
  2. Diodes
  3. Capacitors
  4. LM 317
  5. Potentiometers

Team Mentors

  1. Sreeja Vasantham
  2. Jayesh Hannurkar

What will you learn?

  • Working of the transformers.
  • To change the pattern of an ac voltage.
  • To stabilize the output voltage.
  • To regulate the voltage.

How will we achieve it?

  • A transformer will be used to change the voltage level from 220V to 12V, keeping the power constant.
  • Stepped down AC voltage will be converted to a unipolar waveform with a rectifier circuit using diodes.
  • Capacitors will be used to stabilize the voltage obtained.
  • LM317 IC and a potentiometer will be used to regulate the voltage.

Further!!

  • We can try to reduce the internal resistance of the voltage source.
  • Achieve better resolution.
  • And, in general optimize and fine-tune the circuit.
